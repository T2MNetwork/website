## Venue

The conference will take place at the Hélène-Desmarais Building of HEC Montreal. This is the new building of HEC Montreal located downtown.

The building can easily be reached via public transportation, by taking the orange metro line to Square Victoria and following the HEC Montreal exit.
The entrance to the building is across the street on Beaver Hall (📍: [map](https://maps.app.goo.gl/BtC2wfZA3CqtHg2K6) ).

![](/assets/2026/hec_2.jpg)

## Dinner

The conference dinner will take place at Le Molière Brasserie Française on Tuesday June 2nd . The restaurant is a short 2-minute walk from the orange metro exit Berri – UQAM or a 25-minute walk
from the conference building (📍: [map](https://maps.app.goo.gl/HpEJ7YN7KvtGLx9d6) ).
